📘 Trading Strategies

🔢 Even Strategy & Entry

Criteria: When numbers (2, 4, 6, 8) are 11.5% and above, place an Even contract in all volatilities (Vol 10 to Vol 100).
Main Volatility: Volatility 100 index.
Entry Point: Wait for 4–5 cursor hits on odd numbers, then trade if it hits any even digit.
⚠️ Patience is key — always wait for confirmation!

🔢 Odd Strategy & Entry

Criteria: When numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) are 11% and above, place an Odd contract.
Main Volatility: Volatility 75 index.
Entry Point: Wait for 3–4 hits on even numbers, then trade when an odd number hits.

📈 Over/Under Strategy

Over 0 & 1: Use 1 tick. Green bar should be 10.5%+, red bar below 9%.
Entry Point: Wait for the cursor to hit 0 or 1 once or twice, then trade Over 0/1.

Over 2: Any digit among 3–9 should be ≥ 10%.
Entry Point: If the last trade ends Under 2, enter Over when cursor hits 0–2.

🎯 Matches & Differs Strategy

Digit Differs: Use 1 Tick. Predict digit with less than 9%. Green bar should be below 12%.
Entry Point: Ensure the predicted number has been hit once before entering the trade.

📉 Rise and Fall Strategy

Rise Strategy: Use moving averages, trend lines, and volume analysis to confirm an upward momentum.
Entry Timing: Wait for a pullback or consolidation near support levels before entering.
Risk Management: Place stop-loss just below key support levels.

Fall Strategy: Use moving averages and trend lines to detect downward momentum.
Entry Timing: Wait for a brief consolidation or bounce near resistance levels before entry.
Risk Management: Use stop-loss above resistance levels.
