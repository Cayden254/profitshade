📘 Trading Strategies

🔢 Even Strategy & Entry

Criteria: When numbers (2, 4, 6, 8) are 11.5%+, place an Even contract on any Volatility Index.
Entry: Wait for 4–5 cursor hits on odd numbers, then run on even.

🔢 Odd Strategy & Entry

Criteria: When numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) are 11%+, place an Odd contract.
Entry: Wait for 3–4 even hits, then run on odd.

📈 Over/Under Strategy

Over 0/1: Use 1 tick. Green bar ≥ 10.5%, red bar ≤ 9%.
Entry: Hit 0/1 twice, then run bot.

Over 2: Digits 3–9 ≥ 10%.
Entry: If last trade ends Under 2, enter Over when cursor hits 0–2.

🎯 Matches & Differs Strategy

Digit Differs: Use 1 tick. Predict digit with < 9%, Green bar < 12%.
Entry: Wait until the predicted number appears once, then enter.

📉 Rise and Fall

Rise: Wait for confirmation on moving average + pullback near support.
Fall: Confirm resistance then sell. Use stop-loss just above resistance.
